Subscription Help

OnClock uses Apple auto-renewable subscriptions. New users can start a 30-day free trial, then continue with OnClock Pro Monthly ($2.99/month) or OnClock Pro Yearly ($29.99/year).

To manage or cancel your subscription, open the iPhone Settings app, tap your Apple ID, then tap Subscriptions.

Privacy and Data

Your shift data is stored on your device and can sync through your personal iCloud account. OnClock does not use third-party advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking.
